What is urban farming?

Urban farming or urban agriculture is growing or producing food in a city or heavily populated town or municipality.
It may be a social movement (sustainability, organic aspect, local tradition), food security issue, nutrition and/or income generation motivators. In all scenarios, more direct access to fresh vegetables, fruits, and meat products through urban agriculture can improve food security and food safety.
